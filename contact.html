Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you come to me?

We offer same-day service when available. For urgent needs, call us directly at 501-232-7423, and we'll do our best to accommodate your schedule.

What areas do you serve?

We provide mobile notary services throughout Arkansas. E-notary services are available nationwide.

How much do your services cost?

Pricing varies based on the service type, location, and time requirements. Contact us for a quote specific to your needs.

What should I bring to the appointment?

Please bring a current, valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, or state ID). Also bring all documents that need to be notarized.

Can you notarize documents in foreign languages?

Yes, as long as the notarial certificate is in English. The document itself can be in any language.

Do you provide witnesses?

Yes, we can provide witness services when needed. Let us know in advance so we can arrange this.

Are your services legally valid?

Yes! We are a fully licensed Arkansas notary public. Our notarizations are legally valid and recognized nationwide. Our e-notary services comply with all state and federal regulations.

How do I prepare for an e-notary session?

You'll need a computer or smartphone with a camera and internet connection, your documents uploaded to our secure portal, and a valid photo ID. We'll guide you through the entire process.
